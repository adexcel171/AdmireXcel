Standard Pricing Context in Nigeria and Upwork

In Nigeria, the average monthly salary for full stack developers ranges from ₦200,000 to ₦3,000,000 ($132 - $1,985 USD) depending on experience, location, and company. (Based on exchange rate of 1 USD = 1,511 NGN as of September 11, 2025)

On Upwork, hourly rates for full stack developers typically range from $16 to $35, with a median of $25 per hour. Nigerian developers on Upwork may charge $10 to $45 per hour based on their expertise and portfolio.

Pricing for full stack developer work depends on the project:

  • Basic website design and development: $500 - $3,000
  • Advanced website with custom features: $3,000 - $10,000+
  • Full application development: $5,000 - $50,000+ depending on complexity
  • Ongoing monthly support: $100 - $1,000/mo

These are approximate and can vary. For our plans, they are tailored subscriptions for ongoing services.
